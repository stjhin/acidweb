Our Services

Strategy




Our process begins with an analysis of existing content and assets, the brand’s end goal, and functional outcomes of the project. We customized our methodology between each projects depending on the result of the Preliminary Discovery Process. Client engagement drives our innovation and identify opportunities.

  • Analytics
  • Customer & Market Research
  • Stakeholder Sessions
  • User Journey Mapping
  • Product Management
  • Innovation Acceleration
  • Emergent Technology

Design

We use the Double Diamond Methodology as the base of our team’s design process and customized it between each projects depending on the result of the Preliminary Discovery Process.

Analysis becomes Insights, Insights become Ideas, Ideas become Experiences, Experiences are continuously being iterated.



a. Explore and Learn

With a human-focused approach, we explore and learn from our clients about their challenges, uncovering questions so that we can generate the broadest range of possibilities for the next phase.

b. Research and Ideation

We based our product on User and Market Research. This allows us to deliver a data-driven and comprehensive solution.

c. Prototyping and Testing

We build real and tactile representations of the solution, where they will go through numerous iterations before being shipped to the next phase.

d. Implementation

We dig down on the nano-scale details and polish them with our Development team. This reduces future technical problem during deployment and product hand-off.



Development

We use Agile to efficiently deliver value to our clients faster and efficiently. Instead of betting everything on a “Big Bang" launch, we delivers work in small, but consumable, increments. Requirements, plans, and results are evaluated continuously so teams have a natural mechanism for responding to change quickly.

Through Agile, we can respond to changes rapidly without derailing a year's worth of plans. This ensures continuous flow of communication with our clients. Collaborating with customers and team members is more important than predefined arrangements. Delivering a quality solution to the customer's problem is more important than hyper-detailed documentation.
